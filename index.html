Symbols of the Republic of Belarus

Each country has its own symbols that they revere.So I want to tell you, Englishmen, about our beautiful, native and wonderful country Belarus!

The Republic of Belarus has its own state symbols - the National Flag, the National Emblem, the National Anthem and not only.

Since 1917, the National flag and the State Emblem have been repeatedly changed in Belarus. However, despite the differences, all variants of state symbols had a similar basis, which allows us to speak about their historical continuity.

State Flag of the Republic of Belarus

The national flag is constantly raised on buildings
authorities and some state institutions,
installed in the offices of the heads of these bodies,
in schools, at polling stations,
it is posted during various festive events

State Emblem of the Republic of Belarus

  The image of the State Emblem is placed on the buildings of government bodies and a number of state institutions, inside the offices of their heads,
and also on the seals of these bodies, on coins, passports of citizens of the Republic of Belarus.

National Anthem of the Republic of Belarus

The national anthem is performed at the beginning and in some cases at the end of important
state and public events, including at the entrance
The President of the Republic of Belarus in office - after taking the Oath of office.
The official anthem was first presented to the jury in 1944.
